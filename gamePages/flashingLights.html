About AFLRP

A message from the CEO

Hello and welcome, to the website of Aussie Flashing Lights Role Play, this server for the game ‘Flashing Lights’ all began as a merger between two servers; ‘Flashing Lights RP,’ ‘Aussie Games’ owned by SamSam and OfficerGalaxy respectively, to have an enthusiastic member base that shares a common passion of role-playing as emergency service personal on the game Flashing Lights.

We have competent Community Support Officers, are continually accepting new members and adopting new technologies, with our most recent being the introduction of a Computer Aided Dispatch (CAD) system courtesy of DCRS Online, if you are ready to take the plunge, please feel free to join our community by heading over to ‘Join Us’ on the navigation bar.

Thanks, SamSam, Founder of AFLRP.
