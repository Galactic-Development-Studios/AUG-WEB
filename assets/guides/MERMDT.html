How to make an E-Report.



  1. First set your call to "code 4".
  2. Go to your MDT and in the top right press your name.
  3. On the left press Officers.
  4. Press New E-Report or go to https://dcrscad.com/report-wizard.php.
  5. Copy your call ID from the MDT into the call ID sections.
  6. Write what happened in the call, anyone injured who was involved, etc.
  7. When you are done type your name into Electronic Signature and press, Submit
  8. After that, you can go onto your next call.
