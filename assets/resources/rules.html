Aussie Games Rules

The rules of AUG Community



  • R1: Be civilized and polite, don't cause unnecessary drama.
  • R2: Only use English in text channels
  • R3: No bad language, abuse of spoilers or symbols (i.e. covering a part so it looks like a swear word)
  • R4: No NSFW content
  • R5: No discrimination or hate speech of any kind. No racism, sexism, transphobia, homophobia, ableism etc
  • R6: Any posts which are considered Spam, Malicious or harmful, promoting or selling goods/services or promoting illegal activities, pyramid schemes, or reward schemes are not allowed and will be removed. Any questionable posts/links may be removed without warning
  • R7: Use the appropriate channel, and use it for what it is intended
  • R8: Don't post any adertisements in any channel and don't DM advertise.
  • R9: Don't send private messages about game related topics that can be discussed publicly, please be considerate when DM'ing members of the @Community Support Officer, @Group Moderator & @Group Manager teams. Any threatening behaviour or harassment will result in action taken with Discords safety team
  • R10: Take your personal issues into private chat
  • R11: Don't impersonate others, including Bots
  • R12: Don't discuss politics, religion
  • R13: Do not post advertisements for services, games or servers unrelated to Aussie Games. When posting community content, please keep this related to Aussie Games where possible.
  • R14: Do not use or copy AFLRP resources, like mod packs without permission.
  • R15: No inappropriate user names or status, these will be dealt with immediately and harshly.
  • R16: Any threats, agression or rudeness towards the @Community Support Officer, @Group Moderator & @Group Manager teams will result in an immidiate kick, keep in mind they are a voluntary position.
  • R17: Only ping members with the @Owner and @Community Support Officer roles, If you deem it to be absolutely necessary. Also try not to ping the whole team and only ping a member of the CSO team who is online. Please try your best to not ping multiple members of the CSO team.
